Winter in Missoula offers a wealth of outdoor activities and seasonal delights for locals and visitors alike. Skiing and snowboarding enthusiasts can hit the slopes at nearby Snowbowl Ski Area, which offers stunning views of the Missoula Valley and challenging terrain for all skill levels. Cross-country skiers and snowshoers can explore the pristine trails in Pattee Canyon or the Rattlesnake National Recreation Area, immersing themselves in the tranquility of the winter landscape.
For those seeking a more leisurely winter experience, ice skating at the Glacier Ice Rink or sledding at one of the local parks are popular options. Additionally, the city's vibrant downtown area comes alive with holiday festivities, including the annual Parade of Lights and Winter Brewfest, where visitors can sample local craft beers and enjoy live music.
Nature lovers can embark on wildlife viewing excursions in the surrounding wilderness areas, where they may spot elk, deer, and other native species against the backdrop of snow-covered forests. And for a cozy retreat from the cold, visitors can warm up with a hot beverage at one of Missoula's charming cafes or enjoy a relaxing soak in the natural hot springs at nearby Lolo Hot Springs Resort. With its diverse array of winter activities, Missoula offers something for everyone to enjoy during the snowy season.
