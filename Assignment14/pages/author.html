David Goggins is a former Navy SEAL, ultramarathon runner, ultra-distance cyclist, triathlete, and motivational speaker. He's known for his extreme endurance feats and his relentless pursuit of self-improvement. Goggins grew up in a challenging environment, facing poverty, racism, and physical abuse. Despite these obstacles, he transformed himself from an overweight exterminator into one of the world's fittest endurance athletes.
