How to Successfully Pitch a Tent

Instructable

What is needed

  • Flat surface
    • Body of the tent
    • tent polesr
  • Hammer

Step 1

Check the area around the campsite to find a proper flat place to set up the tent

Step 2: Lay the body of the tent out flat having the entrance facing the direction you would like

Step 3:Inputing the poles through the tent loops

Make sure you have a the poles layed out and assembled

  • Loop the poles through the loops on the tent
  • Should look like an ex once complete
  • Keep tent flat on the ground

Step 4: Raising the tent

Take the ends of the tent and insert them into the holes at the corners of the tent

  • There should be a click once the end is locked in
  • This will raise the tent up

Step 5: Staking Down the Tent

You can move the tent around to change directions on the entrance

  • Take the stakes and hammer them into the ground in all four corners.
