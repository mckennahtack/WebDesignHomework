Books to Read

Verity by Colleen Hoover is a psychological thriller novel that follows Lowen Ashleigh, a struggling writer, who is offered a lucrative opportunity to complete the remaining books in a successful series after the original author, Verity Crawford, becomes incapacitated. Lowen delves into Verity's home to gather material and discovers an autobiography written by Verity herself, revealing disturbing truths about her past and her family. As Lowen uncovers more about Verity's life, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets, manipulation, and obsession. The novel explores themes of truth, deception, and the blurred lines between fiction and reality, leading to a gripping and suspenseful narrative
All Your Perfects:by Colleen Hoover is a contemporary romance novel that intertwines past and present narratives to tell the story of Quinn and Graham's relationship. In the present timeline, Quinn and Graham are struggling with infertility and the strain it puts on their marriage. The novel delves into the emotional complexities of their journey, including feelings of inadequacy, guilt, and the impact of past traumas. Through alternating chapters, readers also witness the early stages of Quinn and Graham's relationship, highlighting the moments that shaped their love for each other. As they navigate the challenges of infertility, Quinn and Graham must confront their pasts and redefine what it means to have a perfect relationship. The novel ultimately delivers a poignant exploration of love, forgiveness, and the resilience of the human spirit.
