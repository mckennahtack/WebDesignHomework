Step one to refurbishing a dresser

Prepping a dresser to be refurbished. Wipe the dresser and the inside of the dresser. Make sure it is clean and dry before step one.

Step 1

  • Take medium grade sand paper and lightly sand the dresser down. Make sure to get all the excess pain or stain off pryer.
    • Once the dresser is completely sand move on to the dresser drawers.
  • Once all the sanding is done. Wipe down both the dresser and drawers to get the excess dust.

Step Two

Now to stain the dresser make sure to put gloves on before handling stain. Then you will dip a paintbrush into the stain. Wait for the stain to dry and then add a second layer. Once both layers dry you can move onto the next step.

Step 3

Last step would be putting the clear coat on the dresser. Make sure to wear gloves. Paint the entire dresser including the drawers.
