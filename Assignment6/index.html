Delicious Mouth Watering Brownie Recipe


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder, sifted if lumpy
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar, sifted if lumpy
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3/4 teaspoons sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup canola oil or extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 335' Lightly spray 8x8 pan
  2. In a bowl mix sugar,flour,powdered sugar, cocoa powder, chocolate chips, and salt
  3. In a separate bowl mix eggs, olive oil, water, and vanilla
  4. Mix dry mix into the liquid mix and stir until combined
  5. ok thats good, now add the chocolate chips
  6. Pour batter into pan and spread evenly
  7. Bake for 40 to 48 minutes, or until toothpick comes out with very little mix
  8. Let cool before slicing
  9. Serve and enjoy!
